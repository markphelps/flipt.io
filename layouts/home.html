On-Prem

Flipt can be deployed within your existing infrastructure so that you don't have to worry about your information being sent to a third party.

Experiment

Use simple on/off flags to toggle functionality in your applications, allowing you to rollout features over time.

User Targeting

Flipt allows you to create advanced distribution rules to target specific segments of users based on custom properties that you define.

Secure

HTTPS Support. No data leaves your servers and you don't have to open your systems to the outside world. Everything runs within your existing infrastructure.

Fast

Optimized for performance. Since Flipt is co-located with your existing services, there's no excessive latency to slow down your applications.

Easy Integration

Simple REST API. Native GRPC client SDKs available in Go, Ruby, Java, Python and many more.
